About

I work to harness machine learning and data science to drive sustainability efforts and social good. Currently, I am exploring how to use volunteer-collected plant photographs to build effective machine learning models for biodiversity monitoring. I am pursuing an M.S. in Computer Science, specializing in Artificial Intelligence, and a B.S. in Electrical Engineering with a focus on Information Systems & Science at Stanford University. Additionally, I am working towards a Notation in Science Communication and serve as a Course Assistant in the Computer Science department.

Post-graduation, I will begin my PhD in EECS at MIT where I will be advised my Sara Beery and Sherrie Wang.
