About This Portfolio

This portfolio is my capstone for my Notation in Science Communication at Stanford University. As a part of this Notation, I built skills to effectively communicate data and scientific findings to a variety of audiences. My portfolio showcases my work across education, equity, and sustainability. Each pathway demonstrates how I've not only gained knowledge but also applied it to create meaningful artifacts to effectively communicate my work.

Educate

Learn → Understand → Teach

This section shows my personal growth from completing capstone projects in my engineering degree to creating pedagocial resources for educators to designing lesson plans communicating college-level STEM material.

Uplift

Observe → Design → Act

This section showcases projects working towards positive impact on societally important problems, starting with careful observation, followed by intentional design, and culminating in action and implementation of the solution.

Sustain

Plant → Grow → Bloom → Pollinate

This section shows my growth as a sustainability researcher, planting by exploring new ideas, growing by conducting rigorous studies, blooming by sharing my contributions, and pollinating by spreading ideas to inspire future work.
